Hi, I’m Aditya Varshney. Welcome to my digital home. This website is my personal blog for writing reflections and things that I am interested in.

I work as a Business Architecture Consultant at **Accenture**. I specialize in leading projects through the complete end-to-end software development lifecycle spanning requirements gathering, solution design, development, testing, and deployment. My technical experience encompasses both custom-built web and mobile applications as well as enterprise packaged solutions. Currently, working with technology stacks that include Oracle HCM, Oracle OFS, Oracle WACS, and WorkForce Software.

I completed my Bachelors in Computer Science and Minor in Business from the Chinese University of Hong Kong (CUHK), where I participated in an exchange program at the Nanyang Technological University, Singapore and an entrepreneurship training at the University of Cambridge. I graduated as the Co-Founder of 2 start-up projects, and Founder & President of the International Student Association of S.H. Ho College.
